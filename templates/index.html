Emulator Tips

  • Click app icons to open live mini-applications!
  • Open Settings inside EbiUI to toggle Dark Mode, change accents, and change wallpapers.
  • Status bar shows live clock. Click the clock widget or weather card!
  • Click the battery icon in the top right to simulate charging!
  • Switch language tags globally in the status bar or Settings.
  • Notice: This is a web-based design simulation. Do not download any files or apps to your local computer; simply click, view, and interact with the UI online.
